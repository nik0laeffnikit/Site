Aquarium Simulator:
Welcome to the Aquarist Simulator game. Here you will be engaged in the cultivation and sale of ornamental fish for the aquarium: Grow fish and get paid for it; Discover new types of fish; Buy new aquariums; Equip your apartment with decorative elements; Get rich.
Hacker Simulator:
Become the best hacker in the world! Welcome to the hacker simulator, here you have to stay in the role of a hacker and move up the career ladder of hackers in the world: Go out on hacking missions by playing games and get cash rewards and a reputation in the hacker community; Buy PC components and increase its power; Use the reputation you get to buy community attributes. The game features 8 mini-games, from simple clicker to more complex games.
Genshin Wish Sim: Artifacts:
Genshin Impact artifact simulator. Collect the artifacts! One of the first artifact simulator, as close as possible to the artifacts in the Genshin Impact game in terms of mechanics and interface. Here you can: Collect artifacts from all the dungeons (and not only) that exist in the game at the moment; Improve the collected artifacts; Store your most successful artifacts; Earn in-game currency to upgrade and collect artifacts.
